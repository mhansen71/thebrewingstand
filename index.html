The Brewing Stand

An interactive guide to all things potions

Potions in Minecraft are magical liquids that grant players various temporary effects when consumed or thrown. Brewed using a brewing stand (left), water bottles, and specific ingredients like nether wart and blaze powder, potions can enhance abilities—such as speed, strength, and night vision—or provide defensive benefits like fire resistance and healing. Some potions can be turned into splash or lingering variants, allowing them to affect others or remain on the ground. Mastering potion brewing adds a strategic layer to exploration, combat, and survival.
